Success Criteria — Version 3

What "good" looks like  ·  How we measure it  ·  Owners   |   June 2026   |   Owner: Love Offei

1  Purpose

A single shared tool that lets the Customer Experience team log every customer complaint, route it to the right Responsible Party, and report up to Management four times a day (9 AM · 12 PM · 4 PM · 5 PM COB) — replacing scattered WhatsApp messages, Excel sheets and email threads with one synced source of truth. Extended with team roster management, agent performance tracking, and coverage gap detection.

3  Core Success Criteria

  • Every complaint logged within 5 minutes — no more Excel side-files.
  • One source of truth — Manager, all 5 Agents and Daily Email Reports read from the same SharePoint list via Power Automate.
  • Every ticket has a Responsible Party assigned — zero unassigned at COB.
  • ≥ 80% same-day resolution rate (rolling 7-day average) tracked live on Manager Dashboard and Agent Leaderboard.
  • 9 AM, 12 PM, 4 PM and 5 PM COB reports sent to Management on time — generated and copied to Outlook within 10 minutes of each slot.
  • Agent slot reports posted to WhatsApp at every Weekday 2 PM / 5 PM and Weekend 1 PM / 4 PM checkpoint.
  • GM Email report auto-sent at 9 AM · 12 PM · 4 PM · 5 PM COB via Power Automate — fully formatted HTML direct to Outlook from shared mailbox.
  • No data loss — tickets survive browser refresh, device swap, and offline pockets (local cache + cloud sync). All files are fully standalone HTML.
  • No credentials in source code — all data stays within the company’s Microsoft 365 environment.

4  Functional & Operational Checks

  • Managers Hub: log, edit, bulk-update, sort, filter, search every complaint; export JSON, CSV and Excel.
  • Team Roster & Shift Calendar — Manager sets shifts on a monthly Sun–Sat calendar; agents see read-only copy with CSV import fallback.
  • Daily Email Report: Grand Total column on all 4 slots; Outlook copy uses bgcolor headers that never turn black on paste.
  • Works on Android, iOS and desktop browsers — no app install, no backend, no IT ticket.
  • Failure is visible — sync errors show a banner; no silent data drops. Coverage gaps flagged 14 days in advance.
  • Performance Leaderboard on Agent Hub — ranked MTD resolution rates with bars, medals, and live ON/OFF shift badge.
  • Cumulative report rolls up tickets by Responsible Party with % resolution; party list is editable in-app.
  • Slot Reports produce a one-click WhatsApp message, styled PDF and 4-sheet Excel export.
  • Zero extra cost — runs entirely on Microsoft 365 (Power Automate + SharePoint) already licensed by the company.
  • MTD Performance Leaderboard — ranked agent resolution rates with bars, medals, shift status badge; synced across hubs.
  • Monthly Shift Calendar — manager sets shifts; calendar synced to Agent Hub in real time; coverage gaps flagged 14 days ahead.
  • Cumulative Tracker — running totals by Responsible Party across all months with % resolution rate.
